1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide a better user experience, and supply information to website owners. This Cookie Policy explains what cookies we use, why we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

2. How We Use Cookies

TailorEx uses cookies and similar tracking technologies for several purposes, including:

  • Ensuring the website functions correctly
  • Remembering your preferences and settings
  • Understanding how visitors interact with our website
  • Measuring the effectiveness of our content and marketing
  • Providing a secure browsing experience

3. Types of Cookies We Use

Category Purpose Duration
Strictly Necessary Essential for the website to function. These cookies enable core features such as page navigation and access to secure areas. The website cannot function properly without them. Session / Persistent
Performance & Analytics Help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting information about pages visited, time spent, and any errors encountered. All data is aggregated and anonymous. Up to 2 years
Functionality Allow the website to remember choices you make (such as language or region preferences) and provide enhanced, personalized features. Up to 1 year
Targeting & Advertising Used to deliver advertisements relevant to you and your interests. They may also be used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and measure the effectiveness of advertising campaigns. Up to 2 years

4. Third-Party Cookies

In addition to our own cookies, we may use cookies set by third-party services that appear on our pages. These may include:

These third parties have their own privacy and cookie policies, which we encourage you to review.

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can manage your cookie preferences in the following ways:

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically set your browser to block or delete cookies. Please note that disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Opt-Out Tools

You can opt out of targeted advertising cookies via industry opt-out tools such as:

  • Your Online Choices (EU)
