Our 4-Phase Execution Model

01

Process Redesign & ERP Preparation

Standardize before you build.

We move beyond tribal knowledge to define your future-state operating model. By aligning processes and cleansing data before touching the software, we ensure your ERP is built on a foundation of operational excellence.

Results:

  • Target-state workflows are standardized end-to-end (fewer variants, fewer handoffs, fewer workarounds).
  • Controls and ownership are defined (who approves, who executes, who reconciles, who is accountable).
